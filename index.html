One Week GUJCOST Sponsored Workshop
on
"Blockchain: Developing Applications using Ethereum/Hyperledger-Fabric"


23-30 December 2019, at CHARUSAT, Anand, Gujarat

Register Here

About The Event

The main objective is to understand implementations of Blockchain and applied to various use cases and real world scenario. To understand the fundamentals behind the Blockchain such as main cryptography concepts likewise public-private key, digital signature, hashing algorithms and key distributions. This event will give great exposure with implementations of Blockchain technology using Ethereum platform where one can implement public or private Blockchain application over the decentralized distributed network. To disseminate of the knowledge of Blockchain technology among students, researchers and academicians. To provide practical exposer to design and develop Blockchain application using Ethereum, Google Cloud and IBM Hyperledger. To train manpower in this field is a growing necessity for the country. To discuss the current status of the Blockchain development and research work in India and across the world. To bring together researchers to presents their 'work in progress' on Blockchain and receive expert comments on such work in progress.
